Launch decision register

This workspace now captures the decisions that must be explicit before Loyalty Continuity can move beyond planning mode. The goal is to make launch readiness auditable instead of implied.

Decision area Required outcome
Pilot scope First live cohort is narrow, approved, and separated from VIP memory handling.
Ownership Primary owner, fallback owner, and escalation route are explicitly named.
Channel discipline Message framework, channel priority, suppression rules, and cap rules are locked.
Control point Rollback criteria and post-launch review checkpoints are approved before activation.
Structural readiness Loyalty storage is introduced only through a dedicated structural release, never through an incidental live patch.
