Approval checkpoints

The first live loyalty wave should not rely on individual judgment alone. Define a small set of approval checkpoints so continuity moves only happen after route, trigger, tone, and timing have been reviewed against the current concierge state.

  • Queue-separation check: confirm the case is no longer in active concierge handling or recovery.
  • Trigger-quality check: verify the handoff reason is specific enough to justify a continuity move now.
  • Message-fit check: confirm the proposed offer or message lane matches the guest journey and premium brand tone.
  • Timing check: confirm the contact window respects recent outreach and current suppression rules.
  • Ownership check: assign clear operator ownership so continuity does not drift between team members.
