Dry-run transfer packet

The first loyalty handoffs should behave like a disciplined packet, not an open-ended migration. This packet preview defines the minimum information that should be carried forward when a closed inquiry becomes a continuity candidate.

  • Inquiry anchor: source inquiry id, request reference, and closure state
  • Continuity reason: referral-ready, return-value, revisit window, or relationship nurture
  • Operating context: owner, latest follow-up posture, and final service focus snapshot
  • Risk notes: suppression triggers, consent uncertainty, or unresolved operational exceptions
  • Next loyalty move: first retention review target rather than a full campaign launch
