Message framework

Loyalty Continuity should launch with a narrow message framework, not open-ended improvisation. Each pilot touchpoint should be classed by intent so operators know whether the move is a gratitude message, revisit signal, referral nudge, or value reminder.

  • Gratitude frame: acknowledge prior experience and preserve premium tone.
  • Revisit frame: remind the guest why the next season or moment is relevant.
  • Referral frame: make sharing feel natural and selective, not transactional.
  • Return-value frame: connect the follow-up to useful service value, not generic promotion.
  • Escalation rule: if the right frame is uncertain, do not send. Keep it in qualification or review mode.
