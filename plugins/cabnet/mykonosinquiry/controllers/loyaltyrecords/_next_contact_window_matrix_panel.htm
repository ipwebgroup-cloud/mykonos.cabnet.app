Next-contact window matrix

A continuity system only becomes trustworthy when the next touchpoint window is explicit. This matrix defines a narrow timing discipline for the first activation wave without turning the loyalty lane into an over-automated campaign machine.

  • Immediate review: high-signal post-stay, strong service satisfaction, or active referral cues.
  • Short window: warm revisit probability or lightly time-sensitive seasonal relevance.
  • Standard window: normal loyalty nurture after a successfully completed handling cycle.
  • Long window: low-pressure return-value candidates where spacing matters more than speed.
  • Suppressed window: do not set a next touchpoint when outcome or consent posture says hold back.
