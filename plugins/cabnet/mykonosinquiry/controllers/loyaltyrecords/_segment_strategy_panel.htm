Continuity segment strategy

Before loyalty storage is activated, the safest next step is to agree on a very small set of continuity segments. The goal is not to build a full guest taxonomy yet, but to define a few pilot-ready groups that can be stewarded consistently without confusing live inquiry handling.

  • Return-window segment: guests whose likely revisit timing is already visible from the inquiry record.
  • Service-fit segment: guests whose requested services clearly support a future continuity offer.
  • Referral-ready segment: guests whose experience quality suggests a soft referral or advocacy lane.
  • High-touch watchlist: guests who may justify continuity review later, but should not enter the first pilot.
  • Exclude-for-now segment: records that belong fully in inquiry closure and should not be pulled forward.

Segment strategy should stay narrow enough that operators can explain why a record belongs in one lane, or why it should remain outside Loyalty Continuity for now.
