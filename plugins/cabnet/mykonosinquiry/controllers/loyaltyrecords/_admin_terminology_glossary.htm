Admin terminology guide
Hover the glossary labels and digest headings for quick tooltips. Use the guide below when the continuity wording needs a fast plain-language reference.
Tooltips are available on digest labels, queue history cues, and source-context cards.
Open glossary
Source anchor
The original inquiry reference and guest name that continuity is tied back to.
Source posture
The queue or closing posture of the originating inquiry when it entered loyalty continuity.
Continuity posture
The current loyalty lane, usually the continuity status plus the loyalty stage.
Value signal
The commercial signal for this continuity lane, usually return-value tier plus referral readiness.
Latest outcome
The most recent recorded result from a loyalty touchpoint or operator follow-through step.
Latest packet
The newest internal brief or packet prepared for the next human continuity move.
Next review
The next human review checkpoint for the record. It is not automation; it is the next visible operator revisit date.
Queue-to-history cue
The queue-side mini summary that lets operators read the latest loyalty history signal without opening the loyalty record first.
Manual draft vs inquiry-backed draft
A manual draft starts without a linked inquiry. An inquiry-backed draft is seeded from a real queue record through ?source_inquiry_id=....
