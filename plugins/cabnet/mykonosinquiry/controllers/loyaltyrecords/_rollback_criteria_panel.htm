Rollback criteria

Loyalty Continuity should remain reversible during first activation. Operators should define in advance what would cause a pilot lane to pause, revert to Inquiry Queue handling, or require leadership review. Examples include unclear consent posture, weak guest fit, poor timing, offer mismatch, conflicting ownership, frequency pressure, or signals that continuity contact could reduce the premium experience.

  • Pause when the guest relationship is still operational rather than continuity-ready.
  • Revert when message-channel fit is weak or the contact cadence feels intrusive.
  • Escalate when exceptions stack across timing, ownership, suppression, and approval rules.
  • Protect brand trust before testing scale.
