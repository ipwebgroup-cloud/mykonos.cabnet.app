Intervention matrix

Once journey state, segment, and timing are visible, operators need a small intervention matrix that clarifies what kind of move is appropriate. This should stay intentionally narrow in pre-launch mode so the first loyalty pilot remains controlled.

  • Observe: keep the record visible only for future review, with no outreach yet.
  • Warm nudge: prepare a gentle continuity prompt tied to seasonality, return timing, or service fit.
  • Value extension: frame a next-step experience that feels like a natural continuation, not a generic upsell.
  • Referral touch: use only when guest satisfaction and trust appear strong enough to justify advocacy-oriented follow-up.
  • Do not activate: keep the record outside continuity when service quality, timing, or fit remains unclear.

The matrix should answer a simple operational question: given this kind of record, what is the lightest safe action that creates continuity value without diluting concierge handling discipline.
