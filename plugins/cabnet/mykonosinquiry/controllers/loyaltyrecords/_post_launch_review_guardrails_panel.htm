Post-launch review guardrails

The first live Loyalty Continuity wave should be reviewed as a contained governance exercise. Do not expand usage simply because storage becomes available. Expansion should require evidence that continuity actions stayed premium, correctly scoped, appropriately suppressed when necessary, and clearly separated from active concierge handling.

  • Check that only the approved cohort entered continuity handling.
  • Confirm Inquiry Queue still carried all active-service and recovery work.
  • Review operator load, ownership clarity, and exception volume.
  • Evaluate whether message cadence, channel choice, and offer design matched expectations.
  • Require explicit signoff before increasing cohort size or adding new treatment lanes.
