Inquiry transfer bridge

Loyalty Continuity should be entered from a closed or clearly transition-ready inquiry, not from an early-stage concierge record. This bridge release makes that separation explicit: the inquiry remains the operational source, while loyalty becomes the long-cycle continuity destination only after the handoff criteria are satisfied.

  • Inquiry Queue stays responsible for active handling, selling, follow-up, and close discipline
  • Loyalty Continuity receives post-service or referral-ready records only after the inquiry route is complete
  • Bridge cues are rendered safely even when the loyalty storage layer is not installed yet
  • This patch is designed to prepare transfer behavior without forcing live activation ahead of rollout
