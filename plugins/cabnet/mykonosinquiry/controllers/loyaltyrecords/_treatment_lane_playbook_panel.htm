Treatment-lane playbook

Once a small segment is chosen, operators still need a predictable treatment lane. This pre-launch workspace should define simple playbooks before any storage activation so the first continuity wave is disciplined rather than improvised.

  • Warm return lane: a light-touch revisit prompt tied to a realistic seasonal or experiential window.
  • Concierge continuation lane: a continuity offer that feels like a natural next step from the original service mix.
  • Referral appreciation lane: a brand-trust follow-up suited to guests likely to refer others.
  • Observation lane: records worth watching later, but not suitable for immediate outreach.
  • Hold lane: cases intentionally kept out of continuity to preserve queue clarity and service quality.

Each lane should answer three questions clearly: what kind of record enters, what action is appropriate, and what outcome would count as progress for a pilot.
