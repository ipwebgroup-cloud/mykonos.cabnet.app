Risk register

Loyalty Continuity now includes a visible risk register so operators can identify which launch risks are acceptable, which must be mitigated before activation, and which should block the first pilot entirely.

Risk area Why it matters Safe planning position
Eligibility drift Candidates may be moved into loyalty too early and weaken active inquiry handling. Keep qualification rules narrow and confirm handoff evidence before any live activation.
Offer mismatch Re-engagement offers may not match the guest journey-state or return-value lane. Anchor every pilot lane to the treatment-lane and message-framework panels.
Channel pressure Too many touches can damage guest trust or create operator confusion. Respect suppression controls, cap rules, and consent discipline before campaign testing.
Ownership ambiguity Unclear responsibility slows interventions and obscures accountability. Resolve owner, fallback owner, and escalation route before the first live cohort.
Launch overreach A pilot that is too broad becomes difficult to measure or roll back safely. Use the launch decision register and rollback criteria to keep the first wave intentionally narrow.
