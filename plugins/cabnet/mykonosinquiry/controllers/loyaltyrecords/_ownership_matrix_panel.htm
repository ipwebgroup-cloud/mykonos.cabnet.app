Ownership matrix

Even before storage activation, operators should define who owns each step of continuity planning so the first live wave does not create overlap, silence, or duplicated contact.

  • Queue owner: keeps the active inquiry or post-service case stable until it is truly ready for continuity review.
  • Continuity reviewer: validates fit, trigger logic, timing, and message appropriateness before any activation.
  • Senior approver: signs off on sensitive, premium, or high-return-value cases when escalation thresholds are met.
  • Recovery override: can reroute any case back into service recovery or live operator handling immediately.
  • Measurement owner: records what the team will evaluate once the first pilot wave is eventually activated.
