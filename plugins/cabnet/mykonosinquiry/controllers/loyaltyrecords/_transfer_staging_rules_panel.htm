Transfer staging rules

The first activation wave should be narrow. Only records that have completed active handling and have a clear continuity reason should cross the bridge into Loyalty Continuity.

Transfer now

Closed won, fulfilled, referral-ready, or deliberate return-value continuity candidate

Hold in Inquiry Queue

Open negotiation, unresolved follow-up, pending close discipline, or incomplete fulfillment context

Block transfer

Escalation, complaint, active recovery, consent uncertainty, or operational ambiguity
