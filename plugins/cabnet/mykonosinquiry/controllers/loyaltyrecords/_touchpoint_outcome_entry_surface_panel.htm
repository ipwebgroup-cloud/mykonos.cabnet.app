Touchpoint outcome entry surface

This staged panel defines the smallest practical entry surface for the first loyalty touchpoint outcomes once continuity storage is intentionally activated.

Minimum fields

channel, outcome, contact date

Optional fields

notes, operator, next step

Live rule

do not capture in Loyalty until activation is deliberate

  • Keep the first touchpoint form narrow enough that operators can complete it in seconds rather than treating it like a second inquiry editor.
  • Outcome entry should happen only after the inquiry is clearly outside active concierge handling and continuity follow-up is genuinely appropriate.
  • The first live activation should favor consistency over detail: one clean outcome, one short note, one next-step anchor.
