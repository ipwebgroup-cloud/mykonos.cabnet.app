Pilot experiment ledger

Before loyalty storage is activated, operators should treat this workspace as a structured pre-launch ledger for proposed pilot experiments. Each experiment should define the candidate cohort, the triggering condition, the intended offer or message frame, the chosen channel, the owner, the expected response window, and the success signal that would justify keeping the pattern.

  • Keep the first pilot narrow and premium.
  • Record one clear objective per experiment instead of mixing multiple goals.
  • Link every experiment back to an explicit handoff reason from Inquiry Queue.
  • Prefer service quality and return-value evidence over raw outreach volume.
