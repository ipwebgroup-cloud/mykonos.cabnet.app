Activation readiness score

Before loyalty storage becomes the live destination, operators should be able to score whether a candidate handoff is narrow, deliberate, and reversible. The first activation wave should favor simple records with strong closure, clear purpose, and low ambiguity.

High readiness

Closed inquiry, clear continuity reason, owner confidence, and low operational ambiguity

Medium readiness

Closure is present but retention narrative or next-step ownership still needs clarification

Low readiness

Open queue obligations, recovery motion, unclear consent, or uncertain transfer purpose
