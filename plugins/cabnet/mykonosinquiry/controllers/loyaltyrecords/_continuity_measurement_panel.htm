Continuity measurement view

Before the loyalty storage layer is activated, operators can still agree on what success should look like. This workspace should measure continuity readiness through a narrow set of signals instead of broad CRM ambition.

  • Return timing fit: the guest is likely to re-engage within a realistic seasonal window.
  • Offer clarity: the proposed continuity lane is specific enough to steward consistently.
  • Operator ownership: an accountable lane owner is obvious before activation begins.
  • Handoff quality: the originating inquiry contains enough context to justify a continuity move later.
  • Pilot measurability: the first live wave can be judged in simple win / watch / pause terms.

This keeps Loyalty Continuity practical: measure whether a small pilot is stewardable, not whether a full loyalty program can be imagined.
